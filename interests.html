Professional Interests

I'm deeply passionate about using technology to transform agriculture. My focus is on leveraging software and digital tools to improve farming practices, boost productivity, and drive sustainability in the agricultural sector.

  • Agricultural Technology: Exploring how digital solutions can solve traditional farming challenges.
  • Python Development: Creating practical applications and tools for agricultural calculations and data analysis.
